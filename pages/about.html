About

A student engineer focused on shipping serious AI work.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and full-stack development. I enjoy moving across the full stack — from training and optimizing models, to building the FastAPI services that serve them, to designing the dashboards people actually use.

I'm currently an AI & Computer Vision Engineer Intern at ArcelorMittal Nippon Steel India, where I built SafeVision AI — a workplace safety monitoring platform powered by YOLO-based detection models, real-time WebSocket pipelines, and PostgreSQL-backed analytics, designed to replace a third-party vendor system and eliminate recurring licensing costs.

Outside of internships, I focus on building practical, end-to-end systems — an AI-powered finance advisor, a mess demand forecasting pipeline, a portfolio rebalancing engine — that force me to think about reliability, data quality, and user experience together. I'd rather ship one solid system than ten unfinished prototypes.

I'm studying B.Tech Computer Science with a major in Artificial Intelligence at Vijaybhoomi University (2024–2028). When I'm not building, I'm watching cricket.
